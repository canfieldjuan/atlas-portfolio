# Demo: Watchlists & Vendor Intelligence

This demo shows the core monitoring product: tracking vendors in real time, detecting accounts in motion, and comparing competitive sets — all backed by live churn signal data.

## Why This Demo Matters

- This is the primary product surface, not a report viewer. Users interact with live data, not static artifacts.
- It shows continuous intelligence, not one-shot generation. The system monitors, alerts, and updates as new review data flows in.
- It demonstrates real-time competitive awareness that sales and RevOps teams can act on immediately.

## What You'll See

### Vendor Tracking
- 56 tracked vendors with churn pattern classification (price squeeze, support erosion, feature parity, compliance exposure)
- Per-vendor signal metrics: urgency, churn intent count, NPS proxy, review volume
- Freshness status showing when reasoning synthesis was last computed

### Slow-Burn Watchlist
- Vendors ranked by support sentiment, legacy support score, feature velocity, and employee growth
- Each card shows the vendor's churn archetype and confidence level
- Designed for catching gradual vendor deterioration before it becomes a crisis

### Accounts in Motion
- Company-level signals showing which accounts are actively evaluating alternatives
- Urgency scores, buying stage, decision-maker flags, and contract timing
- Direct links to generate campaigns or view opportunities for each account

### Competitive Sets
- Multi-vendor comparison groups (e.g., CRM competitive set: HubSpot vs Salesforce vs Pipedrive)
- Run competitive analysis on demand or on schedule
- View displacement dynamics between vendors
